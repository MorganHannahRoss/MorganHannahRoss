Te Whare Tapa Whā

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing):

    Morning Exercise Routine:
  • Incorporate a short workout or stretching routine before starting work.
  • Consider activities like yoga or a quick jog to promote physical health.
    • Ergonomic Workspace:
  • Ensure your workspace is ergonomically designed to support good posture.
  • Invest in a comfortable chair and set up your desk at the right height.
    • Limit Caffeine Intake:
  • Be mindful of your caffeine intake to avoid negative impacts on mental health.
  • Opt for alternative beverages like water, fresh juices, or smoothies.
    • Healthy Eating Habits:
  • Plan for a balanced breakfast and snacks throughout the day to maintain energy levels.
  • Avoid excessive consumption of fast food and focus on nutritious meals.

Taha Hinengaro (Mental Wellbeing):

    Positive Morning Routine:
  • Start the day with a positive routine, including activities like journaling or reading uplifting content.
  • Incorporate mindfulness practices to enhance mental clarity.
    • Pomodoro Technique:
  • Implement the Pomodoro Technique for focused work intervals and regular breaks.
  • Use breaks for quick walks or relaxation to prevent mental fatigue.
    • Clean Workspace:
  • Regularly declutter and organize your workspace to create a conducive and stress-free environment for work.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing):

    7 am Wake-Up Time:
  • Maintain a consistent wake-up time at 7 am to regulate your circadian rhythm
  • Follow the outlined activities to create a holistic morning routine.
    • Morning Reflection Time:
  • Allocate a few moments in the morning for spiritual reflection or meditation.
  • Connect with your inner self to foster spiritual wellbeing.

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing):

    Morning Family Connection:
  • Schedule time for a morning chat or interaction with family members.
  • Share moments of joy and maintain strong family connections.
    • Morning Cat Time:
  • Spend a few minutes engaging in physical activities with your cat for added joy and relaxation.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment):

    Nature Breaks:
  • Take short breaks to connect with nature, whether it's a stroll in a nearby park or simply enjoying the outdoors
  • Consider having indoor plants to bring a touch of nature to your workspace.
